About

There are many aspects to engineering: technical expertise, experience, qualifications, etc., but being a mentor and a leader is my biggest achievement. I measure my success by my ability to help others grow and accomplish their goals.

This drive to grow led me to my current position at Benesch, where, as a Project Manager, I constantly push my team to ask “why?”, to learn, and to share knowledge with others. I also enjoy mentoring future engineers. Currently, I am sponsoring engineering projects for the Eugene Ashley High School CTE Program, and mentoring college students in the UNCW 2+2 Engineering Program, both strengthening our connection between ASCE and our local community.

Through my career, I have learned from my experiences that investing in others is even more valuable than any design I have prepared. At the end of my career, I hope to leave the engineering industry better than I found it. I am honored to mentor a wide range of teams, from high school students to seasoned engineers. Mentoring and leading others is the most important aspect of my job, the most rewarding of all my achievements so far, and why I am an engineer.

Ask me more.
